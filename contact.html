Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?
Our standard MOQ is 1 unit for sample orders and 5 units for bulk production. We can accommodate smaller orders for first-time buyers to establish trust.
How long is your delivery time?
Standard models: 7-15 working days. Custom configurations: 15-30 working days. Large containerized units may require 30-45 days.
Do you provide OEM services?
Yes, we offer full OEM/ODM services including custom colors, branding, control panel customization, and specialized enclosures.
What certifications do your products have?
All products carry CE certification. We can also configure to meet EPA, CARB, ISO, and other regional standards upon request.
Do you offer after-sales support?
Yes, we provide technical documentation, video guides, spare parts supply, and remote technical support. For major projects, on-site engineer dispatch is available.
What payment terms do you accept?
We accept T/T (Telegraphic Transfer), L/C at sight for large orders, and Alibaba Trade Assurance for smaller orders.
