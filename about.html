About ECG Power

A leading industrial diesel generator manufacturer dedicated to keeping the world powered.

Our Story

Two Decades of Power Excellence

Founded in 2004 in Fu'an, Fujian — renowned as "China's Motor City" — ECG Power has grown from a small workshop into a globally trusted diesel generator manufacturer. Our strategic location gives us unparalleled access to premium components and efficient logistics.

We design and manufacture diesel generator sets ranging from 5kVA to 5MW, serving critical industries across North America, Latin America, and Africa. Every unit undergoes rigorous testing before leaving our facility.
